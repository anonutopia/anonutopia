What is a country without it's own infrastructure? Anonutopia has many Open Source and free projects and applications which have the purpose of building our own infrastructure and providing us with profit used for universal basic income.

Here are some of them:


ANOTE

Our main product is our currency - ANOTE. This is Anonutopia's decentralized stable coin programmed by using game theory, smart contracts and LPoS (Leased Proof of Stake) oracles - AnonOracles. It is also an investment and banking token. Read more about ANOTE here.


Crypto Equivalents for Fiat Currencies

Anonutopia's centralized stable coins - CryptoEuro, CryptoDollar, etc.


AnonTaxi

Anonutopia's ride-sharing, rent-a-car and taxi app.

GitHub


AnonATM

Cheap, accessible and Open Source crypto ATM that uses our Open Source software. Still under the development.


AnonWallet

Anonutopia's wallet, based on Waves wallet. For now it's only web based, but we're preparing Android and iOS versions.

GitHub


AnonExchange

Our own cryptocurrency and fiat exchange attached to Waves DEX in the background. Still under the development.


AnonOracle

Foundations of our banking system, they use LPoS (Leased Proof of Stake) principles to provide our network with useful outside data, they hold our funds in multi-signature wallets etc.
