What is Anonutopia?

Anonutopia is a smart contract based decentralized Internet country (or crypto-country or decentralized autonomous country) with its own cryptocurrencies, universal basic income, ATM network, taxi app, housing app and other similar Open Source applications. In a reality, it's a suit of Open Source tools, cryptocurrencies and applications for managing a large group of people with common interests in a democratic manner.

Is your software Open Source?

Yes, we're proud to say that everything we do is free and Open Source.

What technologies are you using for your tokens and smart contracts?

We use Waves for our currencies and tokens and Ethereum for our smart contracts. We're soon planning to move smart contracts to Waves too.
